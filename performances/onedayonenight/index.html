YARA BOUSTANY

ONE DAY AND ONE NIGHT BEIRUT

This piece reflects everyday
life in Beirut, depicting 24
hours from sleep to wake –
a journey from dreaming to
reality. When asleep, the
unconscious blends thoughts
and memories to create
shapes and images.
When awake, the clatter
and noise of the streets of the
city confuse and overwhelm
in a tormenting orchestra.

Solo performance
12 minutes
