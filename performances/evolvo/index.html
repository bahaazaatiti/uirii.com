YARA BOUSTANY

ēvolvō

On the human struggle to
adapt and keep up in an
ever-changing and evolving
world, where both body and
mind are rushed to accept
and conform to new realities.
And on the incompatible yet
intricate and inseparable
relationship between nature
and cities. ‘ēvolvō’
deconstructs’ this modern
human, bombarded with
information, exposing the
disappointments and fears
generated from this dilemma.
A mutant warrior, feeding on
the city, thriving to create a
link with nature, with an
animal spirit at the core,
embarks on the search for balance.

Solo performance
40 minutes
